Project Highlights

Our project aims to revolutionize cataract detection and grading using retinal images.

  • Deep learning-based system for accurate cataract detection.

  • Utilization of state-of-the-art algorithms - Retina Net and ResNet.

  • Automation for time-efficient and precise results.

  • Accessible and cost-effective screening for early intervention.
